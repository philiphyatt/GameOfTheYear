The Philip Hyatt Game of The Year Awards

This is a list of all my personal favorite games. This is certainly not a list of the greatest, most successful, popular, or polished games of all time.
Many of the most popular games are not featured on this list. While I probably played them, they likely just did not scratch that itch for me.
No hate to anyone's favorite games. All peace and love.
